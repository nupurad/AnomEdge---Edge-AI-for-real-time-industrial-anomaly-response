# SOP-SAF-004 
## Factory Safety Escalation Framework

### Severity Levels

#### P0 – Critical
- Fire or smoke detected
- Electrical fault
- Risk of personnel injury
- Structural instability
- Hazardous fluid exposure

Actions:
- Immediate halt of operations
- Supervisor confirmation required
- Safety officer notified
- Formal clearance required before restart

---

#### P1 – High
- Active fluid leak
- Conveyor halt due to obstruction
- Equipment instability
- Repeated anomaly within short time window

Actions:
- Notify floor supervisor
- Log event
- Maintenance review required

---

#### P2 – Moderate
- Minor anomaly
- Small contained spill
- Temporary obstruction cleared quickly

Actions:
- Log event
- Monitor for recurrence

---

### Escalation Rules
- Two P1 events within 30 minutes → escalate to P0.
- Any anomaly with injury risk → automatically P0.
- Recurring issue within 24 hours → maintenance inspection required.

---

### Logging Requirements
- Timestamp
- Location or machine ID
- Assigned severity
- Responsible personnel
- Resolution confirmation
