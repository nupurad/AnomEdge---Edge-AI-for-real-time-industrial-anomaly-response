# SOP-LEAK-001  
## Fluid Leak Containment Procedure

### Trigger Conditions
- Visible pooling of liquid beneath machinery
- Expanding dark or reflective surface on floor
- Active dripping from equipment
- Unidentified fluid accumulation

---

### Severity Guidance
- Minor contained spill: P2
- Active spreading leak: P1
- Flammable or hazardous fluid: escalate to P0

---

### Immediate Actions
1. Halt affected machine immediately.
2. Isolate area using safety cones or markers.
3. Notify floor supervisor.
4. Deploy absorbent containment materials.
5. Identify source of leak if safe to do so.

---

### Escalation Criteria
- Leak continues for more than 5 minutes.
- Spill area exceeds 1 square meter.
- Fluid suspected to be flammable or hazardous.
- Repeat occurrence within 1 hour.

---

### Required Logging
- Timestamp of detection
- Machine ID or zone
- Estimated spill size
- Containment completion time
- Supervisor confirmation
