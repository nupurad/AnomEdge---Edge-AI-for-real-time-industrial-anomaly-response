# SOP-FIRE-002  
## Smoke / Fire Response Protocol

### Trigger Conditions
- Visible smoke plume
- Persistent haze near motors or wiring
- Orange flicker or flame
- Burning odor with visual confirmation

---

### Severity Guidance
- Any visible smoke or fire: P0 (Critical)

---

### Immediate Actions
1. Trigger emergency stop of affected equipment.
2. Activate local alarm system.
3. Evacuate personnel within a 10-meter radius.
4. Use appropriate fire extinguisher if safe.
5. Notify safety officer immediately.

---

### Escalation Criteria
- Always escalate to operations head.
- Notify emergency response if fire persists beyond 30 seconds.
- Do not resume operations without formal inspection clearance.

---

### Required Logging
- Exact location of incident
- Duration of smoke/fire visibility
- Shutdown confirmation timestamp
- Personnel evacuation confirmation
