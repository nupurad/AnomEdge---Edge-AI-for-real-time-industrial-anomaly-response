# SOP-JAM-003  
## Conveyor Jam Response Procedure

### Trigger Conditions
- Stationary object on moving conveyor
- Material accumulation or obstruction
- Belt misalignment
- Sudden halt during active transport

---

### Severity Guidance
- Minor obstruction cleared quickly: P2
- Conveyor halted: P1
- Motor overheating or belt damage: escalate to P0

---

### Immediate Actions
1. Pause conveyor system immediately.
2. Clear obstruction safely.
3. Inspect belt alignment and tension.
4. Resume at reduced speed for test cycle.
5. Monitor for recurrence within 10 minutes.

---

### Escalation Criteria
- Repeated jam within 1 hour.
- Visible belt damage.
- Motor temperature rising abnormally.
- Conveyor structural damage detected.

---

### Required Logging
- Conveyor section ID
- Obstruction type
- Downtime duration
- Maintenance inspection status
